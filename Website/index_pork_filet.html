Pork Filet with Green Asparagus

Meal Description

Our Pork Filet in Pepper Sauce with Green Asparagus is a savory and succulent dish that combines the bold flavor of pork with the delicate and subtle flavor of fresh asparagus. The pork filet is seasoned with a blend of premium quality spices and seared to perfection, creating a crispy and flavorful crust. Paired with green asparagus, which is lightly steamed to retain its delicate flavor and texture, this dish is a perfect choice for meat lovers who enjoy bold and complex flavors.

Ingredients

  • 200g green asparagus
  • 200g white asparagus
  • 200 sliced bacon
  • 100g vine tomatoes
  • 50g Parmesan
  • 20 pearls of black pepper
  • 100ml dry red wine
  • 2 cloves of garlic

Instructions

  1. Peel and cook the asparagus for 2 minutes. After that put it aside and let it cool down (don’t pour away the water).
  2. Wash the wine tomatoes and fry them in a pan with olive oil and garlic slices. At the same time sauté the pork filet (3cm slices) in a pan with pepper pearls and hot oil, both sides for 2 minutes. Put the pork in alu foil and keep it warm in the oven.
  3. Deglaze the roast residue from the pork with red wine and asparagus water and let it cook until the rest is done. Roll the white asparagus in bacon and fry it in a pan without oil. Meanwhile, halve the green asparagus lengthwise and add it to the pan with the tomatoes.
  4. Sear both of the asparagus until the bacon is crispy, tomatoes green asparagus are ready (~2-4 minutes).
  5. Serve the pork filet with pepper sauce, green and white asparagus as well as the parmesan sprinkled tomatoes.
