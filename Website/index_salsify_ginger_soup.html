Salsify Ginger Soup

Meal Description

This soup is made from a puree of salsify, a root vegetable with a creamy texture, and ginger, a fragrant spice with a zesty flavor. The puree is then mixed with broth and cream to create a smooth and silky soup that is both comforting and flavorful.

Ingredients

  • 75 ml white wine vinegar
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 700 g black salsify
  • 1 large potato (300 g)
  • juice of 1 lemon
  • 700 ml milk
  • 1 bay leaf
  • sea salt, black pepper
  • P50 g ginger bulb
  • 500 ml vegetable soupe
  • ½ bunch chives
  • 4 tsp crème fraiche
  • 5cm of orange-zest

Instructions

  1. Mix 2 litres of water, vinegar and flour in a wide bowl. Black salsify thoroughly, peel, cut off the ends and place immediately in the vinegar water.
  2. Peel and wash the potato, cut into slices and place in a pot. Pour the lemon juice over the potatoes. Lift the salsify out of the vinegar water, cut into pieces and place on top of the potato slices. Pour in 500 ml milk, add the bay leaf, season with salt and pepper. Bring to the boil and simmer for about 10 minutes.
  3. Peel the ginger and dice very finely. Add the vegetable stock and ginger to the vegetables and simmer for another 30 minutes over a low heat.
  4. Wash the chives, shake dry and cut into small rolls. Remove the bay leaf from the soup. Puree the soup with the hand blender. 200 g crème fraîche and 200 ml milk and simmer for another 3 minutes.
  5. Serve the soup in cups, top each with 1 tsp crème fraîche and garnish with chives. Serve with crispy wheat bread cubes and orange-zests.
