Cabbage Sausage Pan

Meal Description

This hearty and flavorful dish combines smoky sausage, crisp bacon, and caramelized cabbage with fragrant caraway seeds and tangy apple cider vinegar. The ingredients are simmered together in a pan until tender and golden-brown, creating a savory and satisfying meal that is perfect for a cold evening.

Ingredients

  • 1 onion
  • 2 small red peppers
  • 350 g poultry sausage (piece)
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Salt, pepper
  • 800 g white cabbage

Instructions

  1. Clean the cabbage, wash and cut into strips from the stalk. Peel the onion and cut into fine strips. Clean the peppers, wash and cut into strips. Remove the sausage skin and halve the sausage lengthwise and cut into thin slices.
  2. Fry the sausage in it for about 5 minutes, turning,Remove. Heat the butter in the frying oil. Fry the onion, pepper, cabbage in it and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Add 4-5 tbsp water and braise for 10-15 minutes, turning occasionally. Fold in the sausage and reheat briefly.
  4. Wash the chives, shake dry, cut into small rolls and sprinkle on top.
  5. Serve with sour cream.
  6. Tip: This dish goes best with a cold blonde beer.
