Asparagus Mango Salad

Meal Description

Our Asparagus Mango Salad is a refreshing and vibrant dish that showcases the unique flavor and texture of fresh asparagus. The crisp asparagus spears are paired with juicy and sweet mango chunks, creating a perfect balance of flavors and textures. Tossed with a zesty vinaigrette made from premium quality ingredients, this salad is a perfect starter that will awaken your taste buds.

Ingredients

  • 300g green asparagus
  • Â½ Mango
  • Lemon balm, 1 knife tip red chili
  • 100g Feta
  • 1 bundle spring onions
  • Pine nuts, walnuts
  • Olive oil, salt, pepper
  • 1 Lime
  • 3 big leafes of char

Instructions

  1. Peel the asparagus and boil for 5 minutes. meanwhile, remove the core from the mango and chop finely. Allow the asparagus to cool and then cut into thin oblong slices. Chop 2 spring onion stalks.
  2. For the vinaigrette, mix the chilli, vinegar, salt, pepper, lime juice, oil and lemon balm.
  3. To serve, mix the asparagus, mango and spring onion and place on a chard leaf. Drizzle with vinaigrette, lime zest and sprinkle with feta.
  4. Add a few fresh lemon balm leaves for decoration.
