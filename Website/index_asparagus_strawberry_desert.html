White Asparagus with Strawberry Salad

Meal Description

Our White Asparagus with Strawberry Salad is a unique and refreshing dish that showcases the delicate and mild flavor of white asparagus. The asparagus is carefully cooked to preserve its delicate flavor and texture, and paired with juicy and sweet strawberries, creating a perfect balance of flavors and textures. Served with a light and refreshing dressing made from premium quality ingredients, this salad is a perfect choice for those who appreciate the subtlety and complexity of fresh and high-quality ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 200g strawberries
  • 2 sticks of thin white asparagus
  • 1 lime
    • 1 lime
  • 1 bundle of fresh lemon balm
  • 1 bundle of fresh peppermint

Instructions

  1. Cut the white asparagus and strawberries in thin slices and mix them. Chop
  2. Chop some lemon balm and peppermint leaves and mix them with lime zest and lime juice. If it got too sour, add some sugar.
  3. Add the vinaigrette to the strawberries and serve it in tumbler similar glass.
  4. Can be joined by a scoop of vanilla ice cream or some fresh lemon sorbet.
