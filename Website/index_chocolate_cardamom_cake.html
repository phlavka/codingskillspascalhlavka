Chocolate Cardamom Cake

Meal Description

This decadent cake features a rich and moist chocolate base that is infused with fragrant cardamom and baked until tender. The cake is then finished with a dusting of powdered sugar and a sour-sweet mango mirror, creating a luxurious and indulgent dessert that is perfect for a special occasion or a cozy night in.

Ingredients

  • 60g dark chocolate
  • 100g butter
  • 10g wheat flour
  • 15g powdered sugar
  • 3 Eggs
  • 30g vanilla pudding powder
  • 15g cacao powder
  • 1 ripe mango
  • ½ teaspoon cardamom powder

Instructions

  1. Peel the mango, remove the flesh from the stone and cut into 1 cm cubes. Rather one mango more than one too little, because of the mango mirror for garnishing!
  2. Grease the inside of the jars with a thin layer of 20 g butter. Then pour in granulated sugar and pour out again.
  3. Melt the chocolate and 80 g butter in a pan. Separate the eggs. Beat the egg whites and sugar in a food processor or with a hand mixer until stiff. Add the egg yolks and cardamom to the melted chocolate and beat until smooth.Mix the pudding powder, flour and cocoa powder and fold into the batter with a spatula.
  4. Fold the beaten egg whites into the chocolate mixture. Place the mango cubes in the jars, fill halfway with the mixture and place in the oven. Settings: steam programme // 170 °C // 12 minutes.
  5. Tip: For a particularly strong taste, 1 tablespoon of coffee can be added to the mixture.
