Asparagus Soup with Crispy Shrimp

Meal Description

Our Asparagus Soup with Fried Shrimp is a hearty and satisfying dish that highlights the earthy and subtle flavor of fresh asparagus. The soup is made from a blend of premium quality ingredients, including fresh asparagus, herbs, and spices, creating a smooth and velvety texture that is both comforting and nourishing. Topped with crispy fried shrimp, this soup is a perfect choice for a cold winter evening or a special occasion.

Ingredients

  • 300g white asparagus
  • 2 shallots
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 1 bundle of chives
  • 2 shrimps
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 20g flour
  • 100ml dry white wine

Instructions

  1. Peel the asparagus, cut it in coarse pieces and cook it in 500ml water.
  2. Cut the shallots and garlic in fine slices and sauté them in a pot with the butter. Add flour and stir well until the flour has absorbed the liquid butter. Deglaze with the white wine and add the asparagus including the water in which it was boiled. Let it cook for 15 minutes and then puree the soup.
  3. Sauté the shrimp in a pan with the olive oil until its well-done (~2 minutes each side).
  4. Serve the soup with shrimp and fresh chives.
