Romantic Aspargarus Dinner Menu

"Experience the ultimate celebration of asparagus with our exquisite 4-course dinner menu! Delight in the crisp, refreshing flavors of our Asparagus Mango Salad, followed by a luscious Asparagus Soup with Fried Shrimp that will warm your heart and soul. Asparagus Mango Salad

Indulge in a refreshing burst of flavors with our Asparagus Mango Salad! Crisp asparagus spears and juicy mango chunks are tossed together with a zesty vinaigrette, creating a perfect balance of sweet and tangy notes. Served with a bed of fresh greens, this salad is a perfect starter to any meal.

Asparagus Soup

Warm up with a bowl of our delectable Asparagus Soup with Fried Shrimp! Smooth and velvety, our soup is infused with the earthy aroma of asparagus and topped with crispy fried shrimp for a satisfying crunch. This savory delight is perfect for any occasion, whether it's a cozy night in or a fancy dinner party.

Pork Filet with Green Asparagus

Get ready for a flavor explosion with our Pork Filet in Pepper Sauce with Green Asparagus! Tender and juicy pork filet is seared to perfection and served with a bold and peppery sauce, complemented by the crisp and delicate flavor of green asparagus. This dish is sure to satisfy any meat lover's cravings.

White Asparagus with Strawberry Salad

Experience a unique twist on classic asparagus with our White Asparagus with Strawberry Salad! Our delicate and mild white asparagus is paired with juicy and sweet strawberries, creating a perfect harmony of flavors. Served with a light and refreshing dressing, this salad is a perfect palate cleanser and a delightful end to any meal.
